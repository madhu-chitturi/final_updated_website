Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2025

Our No-Data Policy: Our website does not store any personal user data. We provide content and services without requiring user registration or collecting personal information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to our multi-purpose online posting website. We provide job updates, tech gadget information, sports articles, and online application services. We respect your privacy and have designed our website to operate without collecting or storing personal user data.

2. Information We Don't Collect

Unlike many websites, we do not collect or store:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Account information
  • Resumes or job application materials
  • Payment information
  • User preferences or profile data

3. Limited Technical Information

Our web servers may automatically log standard technical information, such as:

  • IP addresses (not stored or linked to individuals)
  • Browser type
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on site

This information is temporary, anonymized, and used only for site maintenance and improving user experience. It is not stored long-term or used to identify individual users.

4. Advertisements

Our website displays advertisements to support our free content and services. Please note:

  • Third-party advertisers may display ads on our website
  • These advertisers may use their own cookies and tracking technologies
  • While we don't share your data with advertisers (as we don't collect it), advertisers may collect information through their own cookies and tracking technologies
  • The information collected by advertisers is subject to their own privacy policies
  • We do not control or have access to data collected by third-party advertisers

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of our advertising partners for more information about their data practices.

5. Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies necessary for basic site functionality. We do not use cookies for our own tracking, advertising, or user profiling purposes. These essential cookies do not store any personal information and are typically deleted when you close your browser.

However, third-party advertisers on our site may use their own cookies and similar technologies. You can manage cookies through your browser settings, including blocking or deleting them.

6. Third-Party Content

Our website may display job listings, tech articles, sports news, and application services that originate from third parties. When you interact with these services (such as clicking on a job listing link), you may be directed to third-party websites that have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party sites, as we have no control over their data practices.

7. No Data Sharing

Since we don't collect or store user data, we do not share any personal information with third parties, including:

  • Marketers
  • Employers
  • Analytics providers
  • Other third parties

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for general audiences and do not specifically target children under 16. As we do not collect personal information from any visitors, including children, no special measures are in place specifically for children's data.

9. Security

Since we don't collect or store personal data, the risk of data breaches involving your personal information is significantly reduced. Nevertheless, we implement standard security measures to protect our website from unauthorized access or attacks.

10. Your Choices

While we don't collect personal data, you can still take steps to enhance your privacy when using our website:

  • Use private browsing or incognito mode
  • Adjust your browser cookie settings to block third-party cookies
  • Use ad-blocking extensions (though please consider supporting the websites you enjoy)
  • Clear your browser cookies and cache regularly

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
